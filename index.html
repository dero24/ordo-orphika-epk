ORDO ORPHIKA

Seattle's Esoteric Rock Ensemble

THE ORPHIC MYSTERY

EST. 2023 | SEATTLE

Formed in the mist-shrouded streets of Seattle, Ordo Orphika weaves together the ancient threads of occult tradition with the raw power of modern rock. Drawing inspiration from esoteric literature, classical mythology, and the haunting beauty of the Pacific Northwest.

The ensemble features Lazarus Thorn (vocals, ritual instruments), Morgana Vesper (lead guitar, theremin), Silas Crowe (bass, synthesizers), and Oberon Graves (drums, percussion). Each member brings their unique mystical background to create a cohesive sonic tapestry.

Their debut album, "Arcana Obscura," has been described as "a ritual in sound" that blends heavy riffs with ethereal melodies, creating an immersive experience that resonates with both metal enthusiasts and seekers of the mysterious.
