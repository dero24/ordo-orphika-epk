THE ORPHIC MYSTERY

EST. 2023 | SEATTLE

A word of wisdom in foolish times is worth its weight in gold. And so it goes with the philosophy of transmutation at the heart of Ordo Orphika.

Genre agnostic, equal parts improvisation and orchestration, and with a mind of cult like inclusivity. We aspire to celebrate the ascent, and perhaps, descent of man, with our little society dedicated to our oldest rebel, the first poet and musician, the great founder of the oldest mystery religion, Orpheus, the one who names the gods.

Join us on our epic journey of self reflection and exploration as we reach to get over ourselves and see the bigger picture of what brings us all together across identity and ages.

Ordo Orphika is a 5 piece band from Seattle, their influences span a number of genres ranging from rock, blues, funk, jazz, lo fi, dance music, and psychedelia. It features Seattle music veterans Ian McKagan (Kings of Cavalier, Morning Glory Revival) on guitar and Woody Frank (Whitney Monge, Map of the Woulds) on bass, as well as Saxophonist (Dr.) Mathew Wooten, Keys player Jon Plaut, and the handsome and mysterious drummer Robby DeRosa from the exotic far eastern land of New Jersey!

What will you hear?

Rich harmonies, interweaving and genre bending soloist, lyrics rich with meaning, symbolism and mythology, both old and new. Diverse songs that aspire to take you on a journey to a higher place within yourself, or maybe just to find the lowest common denominator.

Come and join the cult today! We have snakes!
